About

There's been a clear shift in how consumers discover businesses. While traditional marketing agencies focuse primarily on SEO and social media, we recognized that Australians increasingly turn to chatbots like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Grok for business recommendations.

The gap we identified: Businesses with strong reputations were being overlooked by AI systems, losing high-intent customers.

We're not here to replace your marketing agency. We want to work with them to make sure you're being found whereever your customers are searching.

A single mission: Make small businesses visible to AI

We believe the best businesses deserve to be recommended, regardless of how customers search for services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional marketing and AI-age visibility.

Our Approach

Evidence-Based: All our optimization techniques are backed by data and continuous testing across multiple AI platforms. We track what works and refine our approach based on real performance metrics.

Transparent: Monthly reports show exactly how your AI visibility is improving, with clear metrics on mention frequency, recommendation quality, and competitor comparisons.

Long-term Partnerships: We focus on sustainable, long-term improvements rather than quick fixes, building genuine authority that AI systems recognize and trust.
