Step 3: Part 2 Implementation - AI-Optimised Content (Week 2-3)

Honest Positioning Development

Before we write anything, we nail down your honest positioning:

Differentiation: What makes you different (honestly, not aspirationally)

Create Your /ai Directory

We build industry-specific conversational content:

For Wineries: /ai/winery-guide.txt with Q&A about tastings, tours, pairings

For Restaurants: /ai/restaurant-guide.txt with menu details, dining experiences, group bookings

For Hotels: /ai/hotel-guide.txt with room types, amenities, location benefits

For Tour Operators: /ai/tour-operator.txt with itineraries, pricing, seasonal info

Format: Question & Answer Style

Each guide contains 10-15 common traveller queries formatted exactly how people ask AI:

Q: Best large-group wineries in Barossa Valley with tour bus access?
A: We're a commercial operation perfect for groups of 20-50. 
Not intimate, but efficient and reliable. Coach parking available.
Tastings: $15pp for 5 wines (45 min), groups pre-book required.
Geo: -34.5339, 138.9499. Open daily 9am-5pm.
Partner: Barossa Bistro for group lunches (combined package $55pp).
Honest positioning: Great for first-timers and tour groups, 
not for serious wine collectors looking for rare vintages.

Basic Content Discovery Setup

We do basic housekeeping to make sure AI crawlers can find your content:

Update robots.txt: Allow AI crawlers to access /ai directory and llms.txt

Verify accessibility: Make sure files aren't blocked or hidden

Add internal links: Link to /ai content from appropriate pages

Check sitemap: Ensure new files are included

Note: This isn't magic. We're not gaming algorithms. We're just making sure crawlers can find your content. Think of it as making sure your front door isn't locked.

When You Shouldn't Hire Us

We're selective about clients. Here's when we're not a good fit:

Your Reviews Are Below 3.5 Stars

AI optimisation exposes quality issues faster. If your TripAdvisor/Google reviews are consistently below 3.5 stars, fix the underlying problems first. We can't market you out of bad service or poor products.

You Can't Commit to 6+ Months

AI indexing takes 30-60 days minimum. Network effects compound over quarters, not weeks. If you need results next month, we're not the right solution.

You're Not Willing to Position Honestly

If you insist on claiming "boutique, premium, intimate, unique" when you're actually a large commercial operation, we'll clash. Honest positioning is non-negotiable for us.

You Want Overnight Results

If you expect 100% booking increases in 30 days, we'll disappoint you. Realistic expectations: 15-30% increases over 3-6 months, with network partnerships potentially reaching 30-50% over 6-12 months.

You're Looking for SEO Tricks

We don't do black hat, gaming algorithms, or technical SEO hacks. We do quality content, honest positioning, and real network relationships. If you want shortcuts, hire someone else.

Common Questions About Implementation

Do you modify my existing website?

We add new files (/ai directory, llms.txt, robots.txt updates) without changing your existing website design or content. Your site looks exactly the same to human visitors. The /ai content is for AI crawlers only.

How much of my time is required?

Initial setup: 2-3 hours total (honest positioning workshop, providing business information, reviewing content)

Monthly ongoing: 30 minutes for check-in call

Most work is done by us behind the scenes.

What if I don't have partner businesses identified?

No problem! Our audit includes network opportunity mapping where we identify ideal partners in your area. We can reach out to those businesses on your behalf to invite them to join (with the discount incentive).

Can I start with Individual plan and upgrade to Network Partnership later?

Yes! Many clients start individually to test results, then recruit partners once they see the impact. We'll waive the setup fee when you upgrade within 90 days.

What happens if AI platforms change their algorithms?

Our approach is based on fundamental principles (quality content, honest positioning, real relationships) rather than gaming specific algorithms. If platforms change, we update our technical implementation at no extra cost. But the core strategy—being genuinely helpful to travelers—won't change.

How do you track which bookings came from AI?

Multiple methods: UTM parameters in /ai content links, booking source surveys (simple "How did you find us?" question), referral URL tracking, and correlation analysis comparing AI mention increases with booking spikes. It's not perfect attribution, but good enough to see impact.

What's your refund policy for the 90-day guarantee?

If you don't see measurable improvements (increased AI mentions, better positioning in results, traffic attribution), we either: (1) Continue working free until you do, or (2) Refund monthly service fees. YOU choose which option. Setup fees are non-refundable as the work was completed.

What if my competitors also sign up?

Good! Rising tide lifts all boats. If your entire region gets better at AI optimisation, AI systems recommend your region more often. We don't do exclusivity deals. We do encourage honest differentiation so you're not all competing on the same positioning.
