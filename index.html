What Actually Works (And What Doesn't)

We're Australia's first tourism AI agency that admits what's real and what's marketing theater.

Honest impact: 20-40% of bookings come from network partner referrals. This is the biggest value driver.

What Works: Quality Conversational Content

We create /ai directory files with specific, useful information formatted as Q&A. While your website says "award-winning winery," your /ai content says:

"Q: Best boutique wineries Barossa Valley with cellar door tastings?
A: Intimate tastings for 4-6 guests, organic shiraz since 1970, geo: -34.5339, 138.9499, $25-45 per tasting, partner with Bistro Barossa for wine-paired lunches."

Honest impact: 15-30% increased likelihood of AI mentions when content contains genuine specifics (coordinates, capacity, honest positioning).

Why it works: AI processes factual, structured content better than marketing fluff. Specific details = useful signal.

What Doesn't Work: Technical SEO Theater

Most agencies claim "explicit crawler direction" via sitemap priority weighting and robots.txt tweaks improves AI rankings. It doesn't. AI doesn't prioritize based on these technical tricks.

What we actually do: Basic housekeeping to ensure crawlers CAN find your content (Allow directives, proper linking). Necessary but not magical.

Why we're honest about this: We won't charge you for theater rebranded as "optimization."
