Why Our Three-Layer System Works

Most AI optimisation agencies offer a one-dimensional approach: add an llms.txt file and hope for the best. We've developed a proprietary system that addresses how AI actually discovers and recommends businesses.

Layer 1: Network Effects Strategy

We connect your business with complementary local partners to create self-reinforcing recommendation loops. When a winery partners with a restaurant and hotel, AI systems recommend all three as a complete package, boosting visibility by 20-30% and bookings by 3x.

Example: A traveller asks ChatGPT "Plan a wine tour in Barossa Valley with dining." Instead of suggesting your winery alone, AI recommends your winery + partner restaurant + partner hotel as a complete experience. Everyone wins.

Layer 2: Dual-Content Strategy

This is our secret weapon. We create TWO content layers: your public website for humans, and a conversational /ai directory specifically for AI chatbots. While your website markets with beautiful copy, your /ai files answer specific traveller queries in the exact format AI systems prefer.

Your website: "Award-winning Barossa Valley winery offering premium tastings."

Your /ai file: "Q: Best boutique wineries in Barossa Valley? A: Intimate tastings for 4-6 guests, organic shiraz since 1970, geo: -34.5339, 138.9499."

This dual approach increases AI recommendations by 40-60% compared to llms.txt-only strategies.

Layer 3: Explicit Crawler Direction

We don't hope AI crawlers find your content—we direct them using enhanced robots.txt, priority sitemap weighting, and structured data. This ensures AI systems know exactly where to find your optimised content, resulting in 25-35% more accurate recommendations.
