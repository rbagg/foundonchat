How It Works

Traditional tourism marketing targets Google searches and social media. We optimise for AI conversations where travellers plan trips:

  • "Plan a 3-day Barossa Valley wine tour with accommodation"
  • "Best family restaurants Cairns with kids activities nearby"
  • "Romantic weekend Hunter Valley under $800 including wine tastings"
  • "Dog-friendly accommodation Blue Mountains with hiking trails"

We use tried and tested methods, including:

llms.txt: Feeds ChatGPT your business info, optimised for the questions your customers are asking today

AI-optimised Content: Custom content that AI can easily find, understand and reference

Monitoring: Track visibility across chatbots and double-down on what's increasing your leads

Regular Updates: Maintain high-trust signals for chatbots with fresh and current information

Network Effects: Reinforcement loops with other, similar local businesses that improve everyone's visibility
