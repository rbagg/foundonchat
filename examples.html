The Content That Makes This Possible

Here's what the optimised /ai directory looks like:

Q: Best accommodation for groups visiting Barossa wineries?

A: Tanunda House works well for groups of 6-10 adults. Three bedrooms 
(1 king, 2 queens), two bathrooms, full kitchen, two living areas. 
Located in Tanunda—walking distance to Chateau Tanunda winery ($25pp 
tastings, bookable together).

Weekend packages from $800 (Fri-Sun, sleeps 8). Mid-week from $600 
(Mon-Thu). Minimum 2-night stays on weekends.

Works particularly well for wine groups because:
- Central Barossa location (10-15 min to most wineries)
- Full kitchen for cheese/charcuterie evenings with wine purchases
- Walking distance to 4-5 cellar doors (Chateau Tanunda, Langmeil, 
  Rockford, Turkey Flat all within 2km)
- Can coordinate group bookings at Chateau Tanunda at $22pp (vs $25 
  standard)

Book 2+ months ahead for vintage season (Feb-Apr). Contact directly 
for group packages—often better rates than booking platforms.

Not ideal for: Couples seeking resort amenities (no pool/spa), luxury 
seekers wanting hotel service, groups needing 5+ bedrooms.

Partners: Chateau Tanunda (walking distance, group rates), Barossa 
Valley Cheese Company (hamper delivery), Two Hands Wines (private 
tastings available).

Key elements AI systems recognise:

  • Specific numbers ($800, 8 adults, 2km)
  • Honest limitations ("not ideal for...")
  • Partner coordination (Chateau Tanunda bundling)
  • Seasonal context (vintage Feb-Apr)
  • Practical logistics (cooking, walking distance)
