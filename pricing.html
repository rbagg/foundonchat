Frequently Asked Questions

Why separate setup and monthly fees?

Setup covers initial work (creating /ai content, configuring robots.txt, building network partnerships). Monthly fees cover ongoing optimization, monitoring, and updates. Network Partnership waives setup because we spread that work across multiple businesses.

Are there any hidden costs?

No. All prices include everything in the plan description. Optional add-ons (HeyGen videos, extra blog posts) are quoted separately and completely optional.

What if I want to cancel?

You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No penalties or cancellation fees. However, AI optimization shows best results after 6+ months of consistent work, so we recommend giving it time to compound.

Can I upgrade from Individual to Network Partnership later?

Yes! We'll waive your setup fee if you upgrade within 90 days. Your monthly rate drops to $99/month and you get referral discounts for any partners you recruit.

How does the referral discount work exactly?

For each partner business you successfully refer to the Network Partnership plan, you receive 10% off your monthly fee (up to maximum 30% off). The discount applies as long as your referred partners remain active clients.

What happens if a network partner cancels?

If a partner you referred cancels, you lose that 10% referral discount but keep discounts from other referred partners. Your remaining network partners continue at their current rates. We'll help you recruit a replacement partner if desired.

Do you offer discounts for larger networks?

The referral discount system is our volume discount. A business that refers 3+ partners pays only $69.30/month—that's already 53% off our Individual rate. We don't offer additional bulk discounts beyond this.

What's included in the 90-day guarantee?

If you don't see measurable improvements in AI visibility within 90 days (more frequent mentions, better recommendation quality, or increased traffic from AI referrals), we'll either:

Option 1: Continue working at no additional monthly charge until you see results

Option 2: Refund all monthly service fees paid (setup fees are non-refundable as they cover completed work)

You choose which option you prefer.
