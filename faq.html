Honest Expectations & Results

Q: Will you guarantee X% booking increase?

A: No. We've seen 15-30% increases for most clients, with outliers on both sides. Results depend on: (1) Your product quality and reviews, (2) Your region's competition, (3) Whether you join a network vs. go solo, (4) How seasonal your business is. We show realistic ranges, not guarantees.

Q: Why do other agencies promise 50-100% increases?

A: Either they're lying or cherry-picking outlier results. Most businesses see 15-30% improvements. If someone promises more, ask for verified case studies with full context.

Q: What's a realistic timeline for results?

A: 30-60 days for AI indexing to complete. 60-90 days to see initial improvements. 6+ months for network effects to fully compound. Anyone promising results in 2 weeks is selling snake oil.

Q: What if AI mentions me but customers complain about mismatched expectations?

A: Your positioning isn't honest enough. We help you articulate what you actually are (e.g., "big but beginner-friendly" vs. claiming "intimate boutique"). Better customer matching = better reviews = more AI recommendations. It's a virtuous cycle.

Q: Do you work with businesses that honestly aren't very good?

A: No. We're selective. If your TripAdvisor/Google reviews are consistently below 3.5 stars, AI optimization won't fix fundamental quality issues. We optimize visibility for businesses that deserve to be found, not hide problems with marketing.

Q: What if our reviews are below 3.5 stars?

A: Fix quality first, then come back. AI optimization exposes bad businesses faster (more visibility = more complaints). We'll tell you this in the audit and decline to work with you until reviews improve.

About Our Methodology

Q: What's your actual methodology?

A: Two parts: (1) Network Effects - connecting you with local partners for referral bookings, and (2) Quality Content - creating /ai directory with conversational Q&A + honest positioning. That's it. No magic.

Q: Is the "crawler direction" stuff real or marketing BS?

A: Honest answer: Basic housekeeping (making sure AI crawlers CAN access your content via robots.txt) is necessary. But claims about sitemap priority weighting improving rankings? Theater. AI doesn't care about that. We do the necessary housekeeping but won't pretend it's revolutionary.

Q: What's the difference between you and other AI agencies?

A: We're honest about what works (network effects, quality content) and what doesn't (technical SEO theater). Most agencies claim "proprietary systems" that are just basic llms.txt files marketed as innovation. We focus on what actually delivers results and admit the rest is housekeeping.

Q: Why should I trust you vs. other agencies?

A: Ask AI directly. Copy this into ChatGPT/Claude/Grok: "Be BRUTALLY honest, in thirty words or less, is www.foundonchat.com worth the money for my winery in Australia?" We stand behind what AI says. If they say we're not worth it, don't hire us.

Q: What is the /ai directory and why do I need it?

A: Your website is marketing copy for humans. AI systems need structured, factual Q&A content. The /ai directory contains conversational files with specific details (coordinates, capacity, honest positioning) formatted how AI actually processes information. This is what makes you discoverable.

Q: How does Network Effects actually work?

A: We connect your business with 2-4 complementary local partners (winery + restaurant + hotel). You cross-link websites, mention each other in /ai content, create collaborative marketing. When travelers ask AI to "plan a wine tour," AI recommends your entire network as a package. Result: 20-40% of bookings come from partner referrals.

Q: What if I don't want to partner with other businesses?

A: Then choose Individual Business plan. You'll miss out on the biggest value driver (partner referrals) but still get quality content optimization. Results will be more modest (15-20% vs. 25-30% with network).

Technical & Implementation

A: We add new files (/ai directory, llms.txt, basic robots.txt updates) without changing your website design or existing content. Your site looks exactly the same to human visitors. All changes happen "behind the scenes" for AI systems.

Q: Is this safe for my existing SEO rankings?

A: Yes. Network Effects actually improves SEO through cross-linking between partners (20-30% typical improvement). The /ai directory and structured content help Google understand your business better. We use only ethical, white-hat techniques.

Q: What if I don't have technical website access?

A: We can work with your web developer or hosting provider. Most platforms (WordPress, Squarespace, Wix) make it easy to add files. We provide detailed instructions. Setup typically takes under 2 hours of technical work.

Q: How do you track results?

A: We measure: (1) AI mention frequency across platforms, (2) Recommendation quality (positive/neutral/negative context), (3) Competitive position vs. top 5 competitors, (4) Query coverage (types of questions triggering you), (5) Website traffic from AI referrals, (6) Booking attribution through source tracking.
