Content & Strategy

What's "honest positioning"?

Articulating what you actually are, not what you wish you were.

Example: Big commercial winery? We say "perfect for tour groups of 20-50," not "intimate boutique."

Why? AI recommends you when positioning matches what travellers want. Better customer fit means happier visitors.

Won't this hurt my premium brand image?

No. Honest doesn't mean downmarket—it means accurate.

  • If you're genuinely boutique and intimate, we say that
  • If you're large and commercial, we say that

Premium is about quality and positioning match, not false claims.

How do you track trending queries?

We monitor what travellers actually ask AI systems. Test variations weekly. Track which queries trigger your business.

Then create content targeting those queries.

Examples: "organic shiraz tastings under $50," "kid-friendly wineries Barossa."

Not set-it-and-forget-it. Monthly updates keep you relevant.

Can I see content before it goes live?

Yes. We share all content for approval. You have final say. Usually 1-2 rounds of revisions.

Most clients approve quickly once they see the conversational format.

What if I disagree with your positioning recommendation?

We'll work with you. But if positioning fundamentally mismatches reality, we'll be honest about limitations.

Example: If you're a large commercial winery insisting on "intimate boutique" positioning, we'll explain why AI won't recommend you that way.

Sometimes we're not the right fit. Better to know upfront.
