Why This Minimalist Design?

You might notice our website is deliberately simple—black text, white background, no fancy graphics. This is intentional.

For humans: We want you focused on the content and ideas, not distracted by design. If you're reading this, you're probably here because you searched for AI optimization or found us through an AI recommendation. You want substance, not flash.

For AI crawlers: Clean HTML with clear semantic structure is easier to parse and understand. No JavaScript rendering issues, no complex CSS blocking content, no image-heavy pages that slow down crawling. Just clear, accessible text that AI systems can easily index.

Practice what we preach: We tell clients to create dual content strategies—one for humans (your beautiful website) and one for AI (clean, conversational /ai content). This site demonstrates that approach. The public pages are minimal but functional. Our /ai directory is where the detailed, conversational content lives.
